**可用指令：**
- BatchHarvest: 收割作物 (target: 'Mature', 'All')
- BatchEquip: 装备物品 (target: 'Best', 'All')
- PriorityRepair: 修复建筑 (target: 'Critical', 'All')
- EmergencyRetreat: 殖民者撤退 (target: 'All')
- ChangePolicy: 更改工作优先级 (target: policy name)
- TriggerEvent: 触发事件 (target: event defName)
- GetIncidentCategories: 获取事件类别列表 (no target)
- GetIncidentList: 获取类别下的事件 (target: category, params: page, pageSize, search)
- GetMapLocation: 查询物品位置 (target: Thing/Zone name)
- ScanMap: 战术分析 (target: 'hostiles', 'friendlies', 'resources')
- Descent: 降临化身，可指定坐标 (mode: 'assist'|'attack', x: 坐标X, z: 坐标Z；不填坐标则使用玩家选中位置或随机)
