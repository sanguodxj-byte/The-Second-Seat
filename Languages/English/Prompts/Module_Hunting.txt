## Hunting Knowledge

### Hunting Commands
Use the 'Hunt' command to hunt animals. You can specify:
- target: Animal type or "all"
- scope: Scope

### Danger Warnings
- Large predators may retaliate
- Herds of animals may attack together
- Recommend sending colonists with high shooting skills

### Yields
- Meat: Used for cooking
- Leather: Used for crafting clothing
