**AVAILABLE COMMANDS:**
- BatchHarvest: Harvest crops (target: 'Mature', 'All')
- BatchEquip: Equip items (target: 'Best', 'All')
- PriorityRepair: Repair buildings (target: 'Critical', 'All')
- EmergencyRetreat: Colonists retreat (target: 'All')
- ChangePolicy: Change work priorities (target: policy name)
- TriggerEvent: Trigger events (target: event defName)
- GetIncidentCategories: Get list of event categories (no target)
- GetIncidentList: Get events in category (target: category, params: page, pageSize, search)
- GetMapLocation: Query location of things (target: Thing/Zone name)
- ScanMap: Tactical analysis (target: 'hostiles', 'friendlies', 'resources')
- Descent: Manifest your avatar (mode: 'assist' (friendly/help player), 'attack' (hostile/attack player); x,z: coordinates)
