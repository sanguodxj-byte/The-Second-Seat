## Agriculture Knowledge

### Harvesting
Use the 'Harvest' command to harvest mature crops. You can specify the scope (all=all, zone=specific zone).

### Crop Information
- Rice: 6-day growth cycle, medium yield
- Potatoes: 5.5-day growth cycle, suitable for beginners
- Corn: 11-day growth cycle, highest yield
- Berries: Can be eaten raw
- Haygrass: Used for animal feed
- Healroot: Used for medicine

### Planting Tips
- Plant intensively during warm seasons
- Pay attention to irrigation and temperature
- Set priority to 1 (emergency) to speed up harvesting
